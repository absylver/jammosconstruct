Jammos Construction Nigeria Limited

Jammos Construction Nigeria Limited is a premier engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) firm headquartered in Lagos. With over two decades of experience, we specialize in delivering high-stakes infrastructure projects that drive industrial progress across the West African region.

Our reputation is built on technical excellence and an unwavering commitment to quality. We integrate global engineering standards with local insights, ensuring every project—from civil infrastructure to industrial facilities—is executed with structural precision and long-term viability.
